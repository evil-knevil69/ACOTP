Legal system
civil law system; constitution adopted 1933, frequently amended since; no judicial review of legislative acts; legal education at Universities of Lisbon and Coimbra; accepts compulsory ICJ jurisdiction, with reservations
Branches
executive with President overshadowed by Prime Minister and Council of Ministers; legislative with National Assembly dominated by executive and a Corporative Chamber, the latter consultative and advisory; and judicial controlled by executive branch

Political parties and leaders
numerous political parties are being organized -- the Communist Party (PCP) whose secretary, is Alvaro Cunhal, and the Portuguese Socialist Action (ASP), led by leader Mario Soares, are the best organized Voting strength (1973 election): former government's National Action Party won all 150 seats in National Assembly after opposition candidates withdrew to protests election restrictions
