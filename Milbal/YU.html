YUGOSLAVIA Population: 21,000,000. Military service: Army and Air Force, 15 months; Navy, 18 months. Total armed forces: 229,000. Estimated GNP 1971: $12.6 billion. Defence budget 1972: 8 billion dinars ($485 million). 15 dinars=$1 1 July 1971. 16.5 dinars = $1 1 July 1972.

Army: 190,000.

9 infantry divisions. 1 tank division. 9 armoured brigades. 24 independent infantry brigades. 1 airborne brigade. Several hundred T-54/55, T-34 and M-47 and about 650 M-4 medium tanks; some PT-76 It tanks; M-3, BTR-50P, BTR-60P and BTR-152 armoured personnel carrier; SU-100 self-propelled guns; 105mm and 155mm hows; 50mm, 57mm, 75mm and 76mm anti-tank guns; ZSU-57-2 self-propelled A A guns; SA-2 surface-to-air missile.

Navy: 19,000.

5 submarines. 1 destroyer. 1 9 coastal escorts. 30 mine countermeasures ships. 10 C)^a-class patrol boats with Styx surface-to-surface missile. 65 motor torpedo boats (55 less than 100 tons). 30 landing craft. 1 marine infantry brigade. 25 coastal artillery batteries.

Air Force: 20,000; 342 combat aircraft.

12 GA squadrons with F-84, Kraguj and Jastreb. 8 fighter/interceptor squadrons with 50 F-86D/E and 82 MiG-21. 2 reconnaissance squadrons with RT-33. (A combat squadron has 15 aircraft.) 60 Caleb trainers. 25 Li-2, Beaver and C-47 and 1 3 Il-14 transports. 50 Whirlwind, Mi-4 and Mi-8, and some Alouette III helicopters. 8 surface-to-air missile batteries with SA-2.

Para-Military Forces: 19,000 Frontier Guards;

1,000,000 Territorial defence force (planned to increase to 3,000,000).
