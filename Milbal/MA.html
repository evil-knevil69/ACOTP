MOROCCO Population: 16,500,000. Military service: 18 months. Total armed forces: 53,500. Estimated GNP 1971 : $3.45 billion. Defence budget 1972: 568 million dirham ($124 million). 5.06 dirham= $1 1 July 1971. 4.6 dirham = $1 1 July 1972.

Army: 48,000.

1 armoured brigade. 3 motorized infantry brigades. 1 light security brigade. 1 parachute brigade. 9 independent infantry battalions. 1 Royal Guards battalion. 2 camel corps battalions. 3 desert cavalry battalions. 5 artillery groups. 2 engineer battalions. 120 T-54 medium tanks; 120 AMX-13 It tanks; 36 EBR-75, 50 AML-245 and M-8 armoured cars; 40 M-3 half-track and 95 Czech armoured personnel carrier; 25 SU-100, AMX-105 and 50 M-56 90mm self-propelled guns; 75mm and 105mm hows; 6 Alouette II j III hels.

Navy: 1,500 (including 500 marines).

1 frigate. 2 coastal escorts. I patrol boat. II patrol boats (less than 100 tons). 1 landing ship.

Air Force: 4,000; 48 combat aircraft.

20 F-5A and 4 F-5B interceptors. 24 Magister armed trainers. 35 T-6 and 25 T-28 trainers. 10 C-47 and 11 C-119 transports. OH- 13, 12 AB-205 and 6 HH43 hels. (12 MiG-17 fighter-bombers are in storage.)

Para-Military Forces: 23,000: 2,250 Gendarmerie

including 2 mobile security battalions; 750 Royal Guards; 20,000 Auxiliaries. 3 34
