ITALY Population : 54,490,000. Military service : Army and Air Force, 1 5 months ; Navy, 24 months. Total armed forces : 427,600 (excluding Carabinieri). Estimated GNP 1971: $103.6 billion. Defence budget 1971 : 1,885.5 billion lire ($3,244,000,000). 625 lire =$1 1 July 1971. 582 lire = $1 1 July 1972.

Army: 306,600.

2 armoured divisions. 5 infantry divisions. 1 independent cavalry brigade. 4 independent infantry brigades. 5 alpine brigades. 1 airborne brigade. 1 surface-to-surface missile brigade with Honest John. 4 surface-to-air missile battalions with HA WK. 800 M-47 and 200 M-60 medium tanks (deliveries of 800 Leopard have started); 3,300 M-113 armoured personnel carrier; 155mm, 175mm and 203mm self-propelled guns; Model 56 105mm pack hows; M-42 self-propelled AA guns. ARMY aviation: Agusta Bell 205,206A (26 CH-47C Chinook on order) hels; It fixed-wing aircraft.

Navy: 44,500 (incl air arm and marines).

9 submarines. 3 surface-to-air missile cruisers with Terrier surface-to-air missile and anti-submarine heli¬ copters (one with ASROC anti-submarine msls). 2 surface-to-air missile destroyers with Tartar. 4 anti-submarine destroyers. 10 destroyer escorts. 12 coastal escorts. 4 ocean minesweepers. 37 coastal minesweepers. 22 20 inshore minesweepers. 5 fast patrol boats {Otomat surface-to-surface missile to be operational in 1973). 7 motor torpedo boats (less than 100 tons). 4 landing ships. 1 marine infantry battalion. NAVAL AIR arm: 3 maritime patrol squadrons with about 30 S-2 Tracker. (Delivery of an order of 18 Atlantic started mid-1972), HU-16A, 24 SH-3D, 9 SH-34, 30 AB-204B and 12 Bell-47 helicopters.

Air Force: 76,500; 320 combat aircraft.

3 fighter-bomber squadrons with F-104G. 1 fighter-bomber squadron with F-104S. 1 fighter-bomber squadron with G-91Y. 4 light attack squadrons with G91-R. 1 all-weather interceptor squadron with F-104G. 4 all-weather interceptor squadrons with F-104S. I all-weather interceptor squadron with F-86K. 3 recce squadrons with 27 RF-84F and 20 RF-104G. 3 transport squadrons with C-119 (14 C-130E Hercules to be delivered by mid-1973). (A combat squadron has 12-18 aircraft and a transport squadron 16.) 12 surface-to-air missile groups with Nike-Hercules.

reserves: about 660,000.
Para-Military Forces: 86,300 Carabinieri.
