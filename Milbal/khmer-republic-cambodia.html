THE KHMER REPUBLIC (CAMBODIA) Population: 7,250,000. Military service: Voluntary; conscription author¬ ized, but not yet in force. Estimated GNP 1971: $1. 5 billion. Total armed forces: 205,200. Defence budget 1971: 18,650 million riels (S336 million). 55.5 riels = $1.

Army: 200,000.

9 static and 3 mobile divisions (each of 3 brigades of 4 battalions). 300 infantry andcommando‘battalions’ (companies). 1 tank regiment. 1 armoured car battalion. 3 parachute battalions. 12 field artillery batteries. 20 M-24 and 40 AMX-13 It tanks; 20 M-8 and M-20 armoured cars; M-3 scout cars; BTR-152 armoured personnel carrier; 105mm hows and Soviet 76mm and 122mm guns; 40mm, 57mm, 85mm and 100mm AA guns.

Navy: 1,400.

2 coastal escort vessels. 2 support gunboats. 2 motor torpedo boats (less than 100 tons). 6 various types of patrol boats (less than 100 tons). 4 landing craft of various types.

Air Force: 3,800; 15 combat aircraft.

15 T-28 Trojan ground-attack aircraft. 19 C-47, 1 C-54, 2 An-2, and 1 Il-14 transports. 3 MiG-15 UTI, 10 GY-80 Horizon and 4 Yak-18 trainers. 5 Alouette H, 8 Alouette III, 1 Mi-4, 2 H-34 and 5 UH-1 helicopters.

Para-Military Forces: 150,000.
