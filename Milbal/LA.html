LAOS Population: 3,050,000. Estimated GNP 1971 : $0.25 billion. 500 kip = $1. 1. ROYAL LAO FORCES Military service: conscription, term unknown. - Total strength: 74,200. Estimated defence expenditure 1969-70: 8.5 billion kip ($17,000,000).

Army: 72,000.

58 infantry battalions. 1 artillery regiment of 4 battalions. M-24 and PT-76 It tanks; M-8 armoured cars; M-3 scout cars; BTR-40 and M-113 armoured personnel carrier; 85mm guns and 75mm, 105mm and 150mm hows.

Navy: about 500.

4 river squadrons consisting of: 26 patrol craft and lighters, 12 landing craft (all under 100 tons, most not operational).

Air Force: 1,700; about 65 combat aircraft.

65 T-28A/D light-strike aircraft. 30 C-47 and AC-47 transports and gunships. About 11 UH-34D and Alouette ////// hels. Para-Military Forces and Irregulars: 36,000. 2. PATHET-LAO FORCES Total strength about 30,000 men (incl dissident neutralists). 52 PT-76 It tanks; BTR-40 armoured cars; 105mm hows. The Pathet-Lao are believed to be integrated with about 63,000 regular North Vietnamese combat and logistics troops, and have received arms and ammunition of Soviet and Chinese origin. The Pathet-Lao and North Vietnamese control all the eastern half of Laos, and most of the North.
