GHANA Population: 9,600,000. Voluntary military service. Total armed forces: 18,600. Estimated GNP 1971 : S2.82 billion. Defence budget 1971-72: 39,500,000 cedi ($30,000,000). I.02cedi=$1 1 July 1971. 1.3 cedi = $1 1 July 1972.

Army: 16,500.

2 brigades comprising 7 inf battalions and support units. 2 reconnaissance squadrons. Saladin armoured cars ; Ferret scout cars ; heavy mortars.

Navy: 1,000.

2 corvettes.* 1 coastal minesweeper. 2 inshore minesweepers. 2 seaward defence vessels. 3 P-20 class (ex-Soviet) patrol boats (less than 100 tons).

Air Force: 1,100; no combat aircraft.

1 transport squadron with 7 Otter. 1 transport squadron with 8 Caribou and 3 Heron. 1 comms and liaison squadron with 1 1 Beaver. 3 Whirlwind, 2 Wessex and 3 Hughes 269A helicopters. 6 MB-326 and 9 Chipmunk trainers. craft.
