CUBA Population: 8,650,000. Military service: 3 years. Total armed forces: 108,000. Estimated GNP 1970: $4.5 billion. Estimated defence expenditure 1971: 290 million pesos ($290,000,000). 1 peso = $1.

Army: 90,000.

15 infantry ‘divisions’ (brigades). 2 armoured brigades. 8 independent ‘brigades’ (battalion groups). Over 600 tanks including heavy tanks, T-34 and T-54/55 medium tanks, and PT-76 It tanks; 200 BTR-40, BTR-60 and BTR-152 armoured personnel carrier; 100 SU-100 assault guns; 122mm and 152mm guns; 30 FROG-A and 20 Salish surface-to-surface missile; 57mm, 76mm and 85mm anti-tank guns; Snapper anti-tank guided weapon.

reserves: 90,000.
Navy: 6,000.

4 frigates. 2 escort patrol vessels. 18 submarine chasers. 18 Komar-c\ass patrol boats with surface-to-surface missile. 2 O^a-class patrol boats with surface-to-surface missile. 24 motor torpedo boats. 50 Samlet coastal defence surface-to-surface missile.

Air Force: 12,000 (including the Air Defence

Forces); 185 combat aircraft. 20 MiG-15 fighter-bombers. 50 MiG-21 interceptors. 40 MiG-19 interceptors. 75 MiG-17 interceptors. About 50 transport aircraft, mostly Il-14, An-24 and An-2. Trainers include 30 MiG-15 UTI and Zlin-326. About 25 Mi-4 and 30 Mi-1 helicopters. 24 sites with 144 SA-2 surface-to-air missile.

Para-Military Forces: 10,000 State Security troops;

3,000 border guards.
