troops). 2 armoured divisions. 10 infantry divisions. 1 independent armoured brigade. 1 air defence brigade. 135 M-47, 65 M-48, 50 T-55 and 200 T-59 medium tanks; 140 M-24, 50 M-41 and 20 PT-76 It tanks; 250 M-113 armoured personnel carrier; about 900 guns incl 25-pounder, 105mm and 155mm hows and 130mm guns; Cobra anti-tank guided weapon; H-13 hels.
