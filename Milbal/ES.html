SPAIN Population: 34,500,000. Military service: 18 months. Total armed forces: 301,000. Estimated GNP 1971: $37.1 billion. Defence budget 1972: 54,172 million pesetas. ($879 million). 70pesetas=$1 1 July 1971. 63 pesetas = $1 1 July 1972.

Army: 220,000.

1 armoured division. 1 mechanized infantry division. 1 motorized infantry division. 2 mountain divisions. 1 armoured cavalry brigade. 12 independent infantry brigades. (AH above are about 70 per cent strength.) 1 mountain brigade. 1 airportable brigade. 1 parachute brigade. 2 artillery brigades (1 coast artillery). 1 surface-to-air missile battalion with HA WK. M-47 and M-48 medium tanks; M-24 and M-41 It tanks; Greyhound armoured cars; AML-60/90 and M-3 scout cars; M-113 armoured personnel carrier; 105mm and 155mm self-propelled guns; 105mm, 155mm and 203mm how; 90mm self-propelled anti-tank guns.

Navy: 47,500 (including 6,000 marines).

4 submarines. 1 helicopter carrier. 1 cruiser. 13 destroyers/fast frigates. 8 frigates. 2 frigate-minelayers. 2 corvettes. 1 anti-submarine patrol vessel. 3 torpedo boats. 17 minesweepers. 18 landing craft. 3 anti-submarine helicopters and 1 light helicopters squadrons.

Air Force: 33,500; 215 combat aircraft.

36 F-4E fighter-bombers. 30 Mirage-IIIE fighter-bombers. 70 F-5 fighter-bombers. 55 HA-200 fighter-bombers. 13 F-86F interceptors. 1 anti-submarine squadron with 11 Hu-I6B. About 150 transport aircraft and helicopters, including C-47, C-54, 12 Caribou and 21 Azor.

DEPLOYMENT (outside mainland Spain)

Spain): Total strength: 41,000, including mechanized and commando formations of the Spanish Foreign Legion, elements of 3 divisions and support units. Balearic Islands: 6,000. Canary Islands: 8,000. Ceuta: 8,000 incl 1 regiment of the Foreign Legion. Melilla: 9,000 incl 1 regiment of the Foreign Legion. Spanish Sahara'. 10,000 incl 2 regiments of the Foreign Legion.

Para-Military Forces: 65,000 Guardia Civil.
