NETHERLANDS Population: 13,325,000. Military service: Army, 16-18 months; Navy and Air Force, 18-21 months. Total armed forces: 122,200. Estimated GNP 1971: S39.87 billion. Defence budget 1972: 4,952 million guilders ($1,562 million). 3.62 guilders = $1 1 July 1971.* 3.19 guilders=$1 1 July 1972.

Army: 80,000.

2 armoured brigades. 4 armoured infantry brigades. 2 surface-to-surface missile battalions with Honest John. 400 Centurion and 485 Leopard medium tanks; 120 AMX-13 It tanks; M-106, M-113 and M-577 (amphibious) armoured personnel carrier; AMX-VTT and YP-408 armoured personnel carrier; AMX 105mm, M-109 155mm, M-107 175mm and M-1 10 203mm self-propelled hows.

reserves: 1 inf division and corps troops, incl 1 indep

inf brigade, would be completed by call-up of reservists. A number of infantry brigades could be mobilized, if needed for territorial defence. Navy; 20,000 (including 2,800 marines and 2,000 naval air force). 4 submarines (2 more to be operational in 1972). 2 cruisers (one surface-to-air missile with Terrier). 6 surface-to-air missile frigates with Seacat. 12 destroyers. 6 corvettes. 6 support escorts. 5 patrol vessels. 26 coastal minesweepers/minehunters. 16 inshore minesweepers. 1 fast combat support ship. NAVAL AIR arm: 2,000; 44 combat aircraft. 2 maritime reconnaissance squadrons with BR-1 150 Atlantic and P-2 Neptune. 18 Wasp and AB-204B anti-submarine helicopters. 23 S-58 search-and-rescue hels, being replaced by SH-3D.

reserves: 9,000 on immediate recall.

6 frigates, about one squadron of hels (all types).

Air Force: 22,200; 144 combat aircraft.

2 fighter-bomber squadrons with 36 F-104G. 3 fighter-bomber squadrons with 54 NF-5A. 2 interceptor squadrons with 36 F-104G. 1 reconnaissance squadron with 1 8 RF-104G. I transport squadron with 12 F-27. 30 NF-5B trainers. 3 observation and communication squadrons (under Army command) with Alouette III helicopters, and Piper Super-Cub and DHC-2 Beaver It aircraft. 8 surface-to-air missile squadrons with Nike-Hercules. I I surface-to-air missile squadrons with HA WK.

Para-Military Forces: 3,200 Gendarmerie.
