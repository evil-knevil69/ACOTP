Peru

PERU Population: 14,430,000. Military service: 2 years. Total armed forces: 54,000. Estimated GNP 1971: $6.35 billion. Estimated defence expenditure 1970: 7,463 million soles ($164 million). 45.5 soles = $1.

Army: 39,000.

1 armoured brigade. 7 infantry brigades. 1 commando brigade. Mountain, parachute, artillery and engineer battalions. 60 M-4 Sherman medium tanks; 100 A MX- 13 It tanks; 50 M3A1 White scout cars; some 105mm and 155mm guns. Piper Cherokee It ac; 8 Bell 47G helicopters.

Navy: 8,000.

4 submarines. 2 cruisers. 4 destroyers. 3 destroyer escorts. 2 submarine chasers. 6 fast patrol craft. 2 coastal minesweepers. 3 patrol boats. 7 river gunboats. 4 landing ships. 8 Bell 47G helicopters.

Air Force: 7,000; 91 combat aircraft.

20 Canberra light bombers. 14 Mirage V fighters. 10 F-86F and 6 Hunter F-52 fighters. 20 T-33A armed trainers. (The above aircraft form three combat groups of two or three squadrons each.) 1 photo-recce squadron with 10 C-60. 1 maritime recce squadron with 6 PV-2 Harpoon. 5 HU-16A Albatross maritime patrol aircraft. Tpt and comms aircraft, incl 9 C-130, 9 DHC-2 Beaver, 19 C-47, 3 DHC-6 Twin Otter, 18 Beech Queen Air and 16 DHC-5 Buffalo. Trainers incl 2 Hunter T-62, 15 T-6 Texan, 6 T-34 Mentor, 26 T-37B and 25 Cessna T-41A. Helicopters include 4 Bell 47G, 6 Alouette //////and 9 UH-ID Iroquois.

Para-Military Forces: 20,000 Guardia Civil.
