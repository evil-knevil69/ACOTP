EGYPT Population: 34,900,000. Military service: 3 years. Total armed forces: 325,000. Estimated GNP 1971: $6.91 billion. Defence budget 1972-73: £E649.5 million ($1,510 million). £E0.43 = $1.

3 armoured divisions. 3 mechanized infantry divisions. 5 infantry divisions. 2 parachute brigades. 16 artillery brigades. 28 commando battalions. 50 JS-3 heavy tanks; 1,500 T-54/55, 10 T-62 and 400 T-34 medium tanks; 100 PT-76 It tanks; 2,000 BTR-40, BTR-50P, BTR-60P, OT-64 and BTR-152 armoured personnel carrier; about 150 SU-100 and ISU-152 self-propelled guns; about 1,500 122mm, 130mm and 152mm guns and hows; 40 203mm hows; 57mm, 85mm and 100mm anti-tank guns; Snapper anti-tank guided weapon; 24 FROG-3, some FROG-1 and 25 Samlet short-range surface-to-surface missile ; ZSU-23-4 and ZSU-57-2 self-propelled AA guns.
