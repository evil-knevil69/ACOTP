SAUDI ARABIA Population: 7,960,000. Voluntary military service. Total armed forces: 40,500. Estimated GNP 1971 : $4.3 billion. Defence budget 1970-71 : saudi riyals 1,723 million ($383,000,000). 4.5 riyals=$1 1 July 1971. 4.2 riyals = $1 1 July 1972.

Army: 36,000.

4 infantry brigades. 10 surface-to-air missile battalions with HA fVK. 25 M-47 medium tanks; 60 M-41 It tanks; 200 AML-60 and AML-90, some Staghound and Greyhound armoured cars; Ferret scout cars. (AMX-30 tanks on order.)

deployment: 4,000 in Jordan.
Navy: 1,000.

2 torpedo boats. 12 fast patrol boat. 9 patrol boats. 8 SRN-6 hovercraft. or on secondment.

Air Force: 3,500; 71 combat aircraft.

1 fighter-bomber squadron with 15 F-86 (27 F-5B and 30 F-5E are on order). 2 ground-attack squadrons with 21 BAC-167. 2 interceptor squadrons with 3 F-52 and 32 F-53 Light¬ ning. 10 C-130 and 2 C-140B transports. 1 Alouette III, 1 AB-204, 8 AB-205 and 20 AB-206 helicopters. 13 T33 trainers, 1 Cessna 31 OK and 6 172G light aircraft.

Para-Military Forces: 10,000 National Guard

(formerly known as the ‘White Army’).

•A number of expatriate personnel serve on contract
