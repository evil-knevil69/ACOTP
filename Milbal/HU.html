HUNGARY Population: 10,500,000. Military service: 2 years. Total regular forces: 103,000. Estimated GNP 1971: SI 5.6 billion. Defence budget 1972: 9,717 million forints (5558,000,000). 17.4 forints = $1.

Army: 90,000.

2 tank divisions (1 cadre). 4 motorized rifle divisions (1 cadre). Some heavy tanks; about 1,500 medium tanks, mainly T-55, T-54 and T-62; some T-34 for training; 50 PT-76 It tanks; FUG-M and OT-65 scout cars; FUG-M-1970, OT-64 and BTR.152 armoured personnel carrier; 76mm, 85mm and 122mm guns; 122mm and 152mm hows; FROG surface-to-surface missile; 57mm anti-tank guns; Sagger, Snapper and Swatter anti-tank guided weapon; 57mm twin self-propelled AA guns.

Navy: 500.

Danube River Guard of small patrol craft.

Air Force: 12,500; 108 combat aircraft.

9 interceptor squadrons with MiG-19 and MiG-21. (12 aircraft in a combat squadron.) About 25 An-2, Il-14 and Li-2 transport aircraft. About 15 Mi-1, Mi-4 and Mi-8 helicopters. 2 surface-to-air missile battalions with SA-2.

Para-Military Forces: 27,000 security and border

guard troops; 250,000 workers’ militia.
