BULGARIA Population: 8,610,000. Military service: Army and Air Force, 2 years; Navy, 3 years. Total regular forces: 146,000. Estimated GNP 1971 : $9.36 billion. Defence expenditure 1970: 324 million leva ($279,000,000). 1.16 leva= $1.

Army: 117,000.

8 motorized rifle divisions (3 cadre). 5 tank brigades. Some heavy tanks; about 1,900 medium tanks mainly T-54, with some T-34, T-55 and T-62; PT-76 It tanks; BRD scout cars; BTR-50, BTR-60 and BTR-152 armoured personnel carrier; 85mm, 122mm, 130mm and 152mm guns; SU-100 self-propelled guns; FROG and Scud surface-to-surface missile; 57mm, 85mm and 100mm anti-tank guns; Sagger and Snapper anti-tank guided weapon; 37mm and 57mm AA guns.

Navy: 7,000.

2 submarines. 2 escorts. 8 coastal escorts. 20 mine countermeasures ships. 3 Oja-class patrol boats with Styx surface-to-surface missile. 15 motor torpedo boats (8 less than 100 tons). 20 landing craft. A small Danube flotilla. 6 Mi-4 helicopters.

Air Force : 22,000 ; 252 combat aircraft.

6 fighter-bomber squadrons with MiG-17. 3 interceptor squadrons with MiG-21. 3 interceptor squadrons with MiG- 19. 6 interceptor squadrons with MiG- 17. 1 reconnaissance squadron with Il-28. 2 recce squadrons with MiG-17 and MiG-21. (12 aircraft in a combat squadron.) 4 Li-2, 6 An-2 and 10 Il-14 transports. About 40 Mi-4 helicopters. SA-2 surface-to-air missile. 1 parachute regiment. Para-MiUtary Forces: 15,000, including border security troops; a volunteer People’s Militia of 150,000.
