Middle East & Mediterranean

Syria

Military Service:30 months
Total Armed Forces:111,750
Estimated GNP 1971:$1

SYRIA Population; 6,450,000. Military service: 30 months. Total armed forces: 111,750. Estimated GNP 1971 : $1.8 billion. Defence Budget 1972: £Syr 960 million ($206,452,000). fSyrian 3.8= $1 1 July 1971. £Syrian 4.65 = $1 1 July 1972.

Army: 100,000.

2 armoured divisions. 1 mechanized division. 2 infantry divisions. 1 parachute battalion. 5 commando battalions. 7 artillery regiments. 8 surface-to-air missile batteries with SA-2 and SA-3. About 30 JS-3 heavy tanks; 240 T-34 and 900 T-54/55 medium tanks; some PT-76 It tanks; 50 SU-100 self-propelled guns; 500 BTR-50/60, BTR-152 armoured personnel carrier; 122mm, 130mm and 152mm guns; SA-2; anti-tank guided weapon; 37mm, 57mm, 85mm and 100mm AA guns.

Navy: 1,750.

2 minesweepers. 2 coastal patrol vessels. 6 ATomar-class patrol boats with Styx surface-to-surface missile. 12 motor torpedo boats (less than 100 tons).

Air Force: 10,000 men; 210 combat aircraft.

80 MiG- 17 day fighter/ground attack aircraft. 30 Su-7 fighter-bombers.* 100 MiG-21 interceptors.* 8 Il-14 and 6 C-47 transports. 4 Mi-1, 10 Mi-4 and some Mi-8 helicopters.

Para-Military Forces: 9,500: 8,000 Gendarmerie;

1,500 Internal Security Camel Corps.

*Some are in storage.
