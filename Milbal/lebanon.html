LEBANON Population: 2,950,000. Voluntary military service (proposals have been made to introduce compulsory military training). Total armed forces: 14,250. Estimated GNP 1971: $1.61 billion. Defence budget 1972: £L2 12,700,000 ($709,000,000). £L3.25 = $1 1 July 1971. £L3=$1 1 July 1972.

Army: 13,000.

2 tank battalions. 2 reconnaissance battalions. 1 commando battalion. 9 infantry battalions. 3 artillery battalions. 40 Charioteer medium tanks; 40 A MX- 13 and 20 M-41 It tanks; M-706, M-6 and AEC Mark-3 armoured cars; 33 M-113 and M-59 armoured personnel carrier; 155mm how; 15 M-42 self-propelled AA guns.

Navy: 250.

2 patrol vessels. 6 small inshore patrol craft. 1 landing craft.

Air Force: 1,000; 18 combat aircraft.

8 Hunter fighter-ground-attack. 1 interceptor squadron with 10 Mirage III-C with R-530 AAM.* 5 transports. 1 helicopter squadron with 3 Alouette II a.nd 5 Alouette III. Some French early warning/ground control radars.

Para-Military Forces: 5,000 Gendarmerie.
