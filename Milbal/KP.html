KOREA - DEMOCRATIC PEOPLE’S REPUBLIC (NORTH) Population; 14,300,000. Military service: Army 3 years. Navy and Air Force 4 years. Total armed forces: 402,500. Estimated GNP 1971-72: S2.8 billion. Defence budget 1972: 1,139 million won ($443,100,000). 2.57 won= $1.

Army: 360,000.

2 armoured divisions. 20 infantry divisions. 4 independent infantry brigades. 750 T-34 and T-54 medium tanks; PT-76 It tanks; 950 BA-64. BTR-40 and BTR-152 armoured personnel carrier; 200 SU-76 51 and SU-100 self-propelled guns; 2,000 AA guns incl ZSU- 57 ; 6,000 other guns and mortars up to 1 52mm calibre; FROGS surface-to-surface missile; SA-2 surface-to-air missile.

Navy: 12,500.

3 submarines (ex-Soviet W-cIass). 6 Komar and 4 Oja-class fast patrol boat with Styx surface-to-surface missile. 3 torpedo boats. 50 high-speed torpedo boats (less than 100 tons). 2 fleet minesweepers. 14 patrol vessels (4 less than 100 tons). 35 motor gunboats. Samlet surface-to-surface missile.

Air Force: 30,000; 578 combat aircraft.

70 Il-28 light bombers. 28 Su-7. 380 MiG-15 and MiG-17 fighter-bombers. 100 MiG-21 and some MiG-19 interceptors. About 40 An-2, Li-2, 11-12 and Il-14 transports. 20 Mi-4 helicopters. 70 Yak-11, Yak-18, MiG-15 and Il-28 trainers.

Para-Military Forces: 30,000 security forces and

border guards; a civilian militia with a claimed strength of 1,500,000.
