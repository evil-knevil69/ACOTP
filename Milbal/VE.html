VENEZUELA Population: 11,150,000. Military service: 2 years. Total armed forces: 33,500. Estimated GNP 1971: $11.3 billion. Defence expenditure 1971: 1,176 million bolivares ($267 million). 4.4 bolivares = $1. 62

Army: 20,000.

2 armoured battalions. 1 cavalry regiment. 2 tank squadrons. 12 infantry battalions. 13 ranger battalions. 4 artillery battalions. Engineer and anti-aircraft battalions. AMX-13 It tanks; M-18 tank destroyers and some armoured cars.

Navy: 7,500 (including 4,000 marines).

2 submarines. 3 destroyers. 6 destroyer escorts. 10 submarine chasers. 4 landing ships. {Otomat surface-to-surface missile are on order.)

Air Force: 6,000; 111 combat aircraft.

15 B-2 Canberra bombers. 15 B-25 Mitchell WghX bombers. 15 Venom FB-4 and Fiamp/re FB-5 fighter-bombers. 16 CF-5A fighters. 50 F-86F/K fighters. (15 Mirage II IE fighters are on order.) 45 transport ac incl C-47; 18 C-123B and 4 C-130. About 90 trainers incl 2 Mirage HID, T-6 Texan, T-24 Mentor, T-52 Jet Provost and T-11 Vampire. About 35 helicopters, incl 20 Alouette II and III, 6 Bell 47 and 4 Sikorsky UH-19.

Para-Military Forces: The National Guard, a

volunteer force with a total strength of 10,000, employed chiefly on internal security duties.
